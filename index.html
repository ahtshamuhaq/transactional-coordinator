Our Services

Our transaction coordinators will ensure that your deadlines are met, paperwork is complete, and they will coordinate all communication with buyers, lenders, and escrow. Our job is to relieve you of this burden so you can focus on growing your business and networking, without having to spend endless hours each year on paperwork

  • Introduce our role in the transaction to every party involved.
  • Ensuring thorough property assessments.
  • Confirm date/time of home inspection and confirm utilities will be turned on.
  • Streamlining the appraisal process for efficiency.
  • Ensuring regulatory compliance and correct appraisals.
  • Prepare additional addenda as needed (price reductions, repairs, extensions, etc.)
  • Review every document to verify correct dates, initials, and signatures.
  • Supply an executed Purchase Agreement to every party.
  • Open escrow, obtain a file number, request preliminaries, and instructions.
  • Verify contact information, dates, and documents are uploaded to our online service where it will remain securely accessible to all parties 24/7.
  • Prepare a Full Disclosure Package delivered via mail, email or DocuSign to buyer and/or the seller.
  • Send copies of all reports to the buyer's agent and escrow company.
  • Upload all reports, disclosures and documents into our online service as it is received or executed.
  • Send all necessary documents to escrow .
  • Track escrow time frames for receipt, delivery and return of all documents and deposits.
  • Maintain constant contact with every party for status updates or to alert you of anything outstanding or requiring attention.
  • Complete and deliver a full digital paper trail (PDF) to you.

What You Can Expect From Us

Effective Transaction Support

Our primary objective is to facilitate a seamless transaction process, enhancing the client's experience from contract to close. To achieve this, our team includes a licensed real estate transaction coordinator with local market expertise, ensuring seamless coordination among all parties involved.

Zero Initial Expenses

You pay only for the transactions that close and fund. There are NO contract termination fees, signup fees, or time-term contract to sign. Our pricing is simple, easy to understand, and services are billed on the CDA or invoiced online. We protect your data, sign non-disclosure and non-solicitation agreements, and are privately insured.

Extensive Recordkeeping and Time Management

We handle all of the important paperwork and deadlines related to the transaction, making sure that everything is finished on time for the closing.

Customer Service

We are committed to providing outstanding customer support, ensuring your needs are met promptly and professionally. We prioritize your satisfaction and are here to assist you every step of the way

Tailored Services

Since no two deals are the same and every agent has different requirements, we are willing to modify our services to meet your specific needs.

Regulatory Compliance

We also promise to see to it that all contracts follow local and state laws. We keep up with changes in regulations.

We are available 24/7 to support you

We respect your privacy and do not tolerate spam and will never sell, rent, lease or give away your information to any third party.
